Quest 1

5•5•2024•5:03am

I did not mean to start the journie this day,

Though the body lost that battle.

I traveled by spirit.

I am not awake.

My spirit soared through the cosmos.

I passed by so many flowers.

The forever sunset, I joied.

It’s calm here; To my shock.

Blue, purple, pink, orange.

A beautiful canvas.

I would like to meet its artist one day.

My first ever journie; quest.

 

It may not be my forte; I give credit where due.

I think the cosmos is wanting to speak.

I dim my sight.

I open them to a very open place.

A blue sky.

Atop of a building?

I am in the modern realm.

Reality.

I no longer crave to inhabit a place such as.

Why are you presenting me this?

I turn to see a person shroud in a very light hue.

Lilac.

My friendly stranger.

I only have met them in the farthest part of my garden.

How-Why am I here?

This is not my domain.

I will like to leave.

As much as I want to; I can not.

The same I know.

The cosmos will not let me leave.

Until I see what is needed to see.

It tells me to let go of what I know; See.

 

I trust the cosmos to not steer me wrongly.

So I do.

I surrender.

 

I am-It is unbelievable.

I can see what they look like.

They-He is immensely pleasing to one's ocular.

Long hair.

Sleepy eyes.

And a very puzzling demeanor.

He is not distressed.

More of in thought.

He looks out in the distance.

What can his gaze be on?

I have not ever been this close before.

I am always far away…

 

I was just a mere minute.

In what felt like hours.

I seen where he dwells.

Yet I did not ask to journie here.

So why; Is my question.

 

I am genuinely concerned that there may be a lesson on the horizon.
