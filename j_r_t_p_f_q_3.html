Journie: Retrieve the Purple Flower: Quest 3

[Time & date forgotten because of my faults]

I have visited the cosmos again.
I hope to see more.
I hope to see the truth.
What is it that I am missing?

I am placed in an area.
Again, the modern realm.
It looks to be a parking lot.

He sits on the concrete.
Talking enthusiastically.
To me?
I do not know.
He looks happy.
Happiness looks great on him.

It is strange.
He is talking directly at me.
Not to me.
Can he not see me?
I am here.
Am I a ghost?
Can I manifest in the modern world?
Or is it his ability to see the dead?

I look around.
The surroundings are covered in mist.
Why am I viewing this?
I do not want to be here,
When the dead is not revered.

I can hear & see.
I am in front of you.
Within arms length.
Yet you can not see...
Me.

I want to leave.
Cosmos.
Why are you not listening to me?
Please.
Please let me leave.

The Cosmos tell me not yet.
The journie is not yet finished.

I tell the Cosmos aloud,
“What kind of test is this that I must endure? From past endeavors, I know I must suffer greatly. Why must you break me completely each time to do so?”
The Cosmos rebuttals:
“I am a teacher of many. The student must learn and conquer. The student can not learn if there is no turmoil. Everything I do is within reason. To learn is to become stronger. To reach higher plains, you first must dig up the roots and realize what needs to be mend.
Once you wake, you’ll understand what needs to be done.”

The Cosmos says that what they do is within reason.
This is painful.
This is torturous.
I have no will here.
I want to go home.

I have known for months that this was inevitable.
I just did not know it was going to hurt this much.
