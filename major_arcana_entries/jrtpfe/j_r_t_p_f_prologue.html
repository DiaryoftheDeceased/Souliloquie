Prologue

5•4•2024•3:05pm

To stay awake or sleep

Is the question.

To fly with my wings.

Or to fly with my spirit.

 

I have to be vigilant.

Sleep is where I am most vulnerable.

One would think being amongst the dead,

Is where it is unsafe.

 

Because of the cosmos,

I know secrets of people I wish not to know.

Yet the cosmos force me to watch and learn.

My will means nothing there.

 

The cosmos is beautiful.

This realm is forever at dusk.

Littered with purple flowers.

The Heliotropes being at the entrance.

 

One can access anything within this realm.

Once you gained entry to this world,

You are gravely rewarded

with lot that is deemed impossible.

 

This place has been calling for some time.

I did not seek out this place; I was invited.

It is an honor to have an audience with the cosmos.

Can you guess how I got the invitation?
